Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

This app stores all task data locally on your device. No task data is transmitted to external servers.

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, your name, email address, and profile photo are stored locally on your device to personalise your experience. We do not transmit this data to any server.

Notifications

Local notifications are generated on-device. No data is sent externally to deliver them.

Analytics

This app does not collect analytics data.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at the project's GitHub repository.
